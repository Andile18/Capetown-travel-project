Why Capetown?

Capetown, the mother city

Cape Town is nicknamed the Mother City because it’s believed to be the first and oldest city in the country, and thus the “mother” of modern South Africa. In addition, Cape Town is also seen as the first European city established in South Africa, making it the “mother” of all European settlements in the country.

Cafès

My favorite cafès in Capetown

Plato Cafè

A lively all-day cafè with a beautiful outdoor terrace that serves everything you could want from breakfast and brunch to any pastry of choice

Address:

120 Main Rd, Sea Point, Cape Town, 8060

What I like about it

This is one of the best brunch/coffee spots in Capetown. If you go at any time of the day, there will be something delicious on the menu! It is also the best spot to work at if you work from home

Bean There

The Bean There cafe method, this warm cosy cafe will take you from breakfast through to dinner with excellent coffee and locally-sourced menu

Address:

Just like bean there,the owners are incredibly friendly and chatty. It has the best coffe in Capetown!

The Charlse

A modest interior, classic furnishings and an authentic fireplace creates a quaint and cosy setting for coffee, conversations, and creative contemplations

Address:

137 Waterkant Street. Cape Town, 8001, South Africa

What I like about it

All the dishes are prepared as fresh as possible. They're not fans of pesticides and preservatives! They do their best to use local, organic, and free-range produce as much as availability allows
